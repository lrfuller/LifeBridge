The LifeBridge Health online media center provides journalists and the media with up-to-date information about LifeBridge Health member hospitals, institutions and facilities. Our staff is available to assist local, regional, national and international news media outlets in answering questions about health-related media stories, news releases and events.

To arrange a media interview with our experts, please contact the LifeBridge Health Public Relations/Media Relations Team at 410-601-5022.

On-call after-hours media help is available 24 hours a day, 365 days a year.
Call the LifeBridge Health operator at 410-601-9000, and ask for the HOC (hospital operations coordinator).
