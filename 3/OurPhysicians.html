Our Physicians - Drs. Gouel and Blotny

Elias G. Gouel, M.D., F.A.A.P.  Dr. Gouel was born and raised in Egypt. He attended Ain Shams University in Cairo, Egypt and emigrated to the United States in 1976. He completed his Pediatric residency at Baltimore City Hospital (Johns Hopkins Bayview) and a Fellowship in general pediatrics at Johns Hopkins Hospital. He has maintained a busy practice in Baltimore while raising his family. Dr. Gouel is active in his church and civic activities. He is fluent in Arabic and French.

 

Natalie Bryson, CRNP   We are excited to welcome Natalie Bryson, CRNP to our pediatric team. As a Family Nurse Practitioner, Natalie can provide care across the lifespan, but has chosen to focus on pediatric care to children from newborns to young adults. She is experienced in pediatrics and critical care, with a passion for providing high quality care. Natalie enjoys all aspects of pediatric care but takes particular pride in the relationships she forms with her patients and their families. Her clinical interests include playing tennis and pickleball, hiking, yoga, and spending time with her husband, son, cat, and two dogs.

 

Lois B. Sullivan, M.D., F.A.A.P.  Dr. Sullivan was raised in St. Louis, Missouri and attended medical school at Washington University School of Medicine. She completed her residency and was Chief Resident in Pediatrics at the St. Louis Children's Hospital before moving to Maryland. Prior to joining Drs. Gouel and Blotny she practiced in Dundalk, Maryland. Dr. Sullivan is married and enjoys running, being outdoors, singing and raising an active family of all boys.
