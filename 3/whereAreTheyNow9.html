Career Choices of Three-Year Program Graduates

General Internal Medicine

Many of our residents choose to pursue careers as general internists, either in the outpatient or inpatient setting, or sometimes both. Regardless of whether they go into academia or pursue private practice, our graduates consistently feel more than adequately prepared for independent patient management. This is because of the clinical training that they have received and an individualized progression from early supervision to a level of achieved autonomy.

Fellowships

Sinai Hospital has a strong track record of placing residents seeking fellowship positions in their preferred subspecialties at distinguished academic institutions. Much of this has to do with the unique opportunities that are available at Sinai and The Johns Hopkins Hospital.
