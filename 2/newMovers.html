Your family’s healthcare journey starts here. Welcome.

At LifeBridge Health, you are family. We see care not as just a service, but a promise to every patient we sit down with, from newborns to elders. We CARE BRAVELY every day, for patients, families and communities.

We offer a full continuum of care, both in person and via telemedicine, including:

  • Urgent care
  • Primary & specialty care
  • Pediatric practices
  • Home health & wellness
  • Skilled nursing facilities
  • End of life care

With convenient locations, online scheduling, and in many cases same-day appointments, when you choose LifeBridge Health, you get fast, easy access to award-winning care close to home by best-in-class healthcare providers across the greater Baltimore region. And because safety is our top priority, we closely follow the latest CDC protocols.
