Breast cancer is one of the most commonly diagnosed cancers among women in the U.S., and in recent years, the number of incidences continue to rise. An annual mammogram screening is important and may save your life.

For the sixth consecutive year, our annual Mammothon event will screen women and continue the fight against breast cancer. On Wednesday, Nov. 10, LifeBridge Health breast care centers and select Advanced Radiology locations will join forces and offer extended hours for mammogram screenings.

Select between our eight convenient locations and reserve your spot. Towson Hot Bagels will be providing pink bagels at the event.

Now is your time. Prioritize your health and prevent breast cancer.

Screening Recommendations
  • Women older than age 25 should undergo formal risk assessment for breast cancer*
  • Women with an average risk of breast cancer should initiate yearly screening mammography at age 40*
  • Women with a higher-than-average risk of breast cancer should undergo yearly screening mammography and be offered yearly supplemental imaging; this screening should be initiated at a risk-based age*

* The American Society of Breast Surgeons
