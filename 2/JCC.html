Health and Wellness Support and Education

Life gets busy; we’re here to make keeping up with your health just a little bit easier. With LifeBridge Health’s exclusive partnership with the Rosenbloom Jewish Community Center (JCC) in Owings Mills and the Weinberg Jewish Community Center (JCC) in Park Heights, members and guests have access to a wide range of health and wellness services. Our Living Well at the J partnership has tons to offer, making healthy living more accessible to our community.

The JCC

Since 1854, the JCC of Greater Baltimore has been a home to the Jewish community, providing communal programs and activities for individuals and families. Their inclusive, caring environment is one where everyone is welcome—uniting groups and individuals for the betterment of the whole community. The Jewish Community Centers in the Baltimore area include state-of-the-art fitness and preschool facilities and programs, as well as arts and culture, health, and Jewish life programming for all ages.

Our Partnership

The collaboration between LifeBridge Health and the Baltimore JCCs began in 2015 with the addition of a full-time community wellness coordinator to assist members with community health services and convenient onsite physical therapy at the Rosenbloom Owings Mills JCC. In 2020, the Living Well at the J partnership expanded and a second on-site coordinator was added to the Weinberg Park Heights JCC. The new Sinai Wellness and Education Suite [link to suite landing page] (located in the Park Heights location) provides a community wellness space for screenings, in person and virtual wellness classes, support groups and events. The Living Well at the J partnership continues to grow and find innovative ways to support health & wellness within the community.
