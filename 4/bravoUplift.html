FAQs

Everything you need to know about the contest


Who is eligible to submit ideas?

A: Any team member below director level and employed by LifeBridge Health. This includes union and non-union staff and medical residents, as well as full and part-time team members.

What ideas are allowed?

A: Any new, independent ideas that have not be evaluated or submitted elsewhere. The ideas must not include requests for new team members or increases to current team member salaries, team member hardship assistance, tuition, new buildings or large equipment purchases.

Can I submit more than one idea?

A: Yes, but each submission must be independent of each other.

What are the rewards?

A:
$50 in Bravo! rewards for a qualifying idea

$500 in cash for each finalist team member (we are accepting up to 10 finalist ideas)

$2000 in cash for each winning team member (we will have 1-3 winning ideas)

What is the timeline?

A:

  • November 16, 2021 (week of): Kick-off for idea submissions

  • January 21, 2022: Submission deadline

  • February 8, 2022: Finalists selected

  • March 8, 2022: Winner(s) are announced

  • May 3, 2022: Award banquet


What is the evaluation criteria?

A: All ideas are reviewed at submission and evaluated for:


  • Feasibility – ability to implement the solution at a reasonable cost and timeframe

  • Impact – the use case for the idea influences a significant portion of our team members or patients; or has a significant impact to revenue, cost avoidance, or patient experience

  • Innovation – the idea is novel and has not been implemented at multiple other institutions within the LifeBridge Health system

  • Alignment – the idea is aligned with LifeBridge Health mission and vision.

What is the maximum size of the team?

A: Up to four team members are allowed for each idea and challenge entry submitted.

Who are the judges?

A: The Innovation Department will evaluate all submissions and identify those that meet the minimum criteria for the $50 Bravo! reward. The Innovation Department will work with subject matter experts from other departments and services to identify the finalists who will receive the $500 reward.

A group of senior level leaders will evaluate all finalists and choose the winner(s) of the Uplift Innovation Challenge and be eligible for the $2,000 reward.

Where can I go for support or questions?

A: Please feel free to contact Uplift@lifebridgehealth.org
